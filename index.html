Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/01/2024

At Sara Motors, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about our practices regarding data collection and usage on our website, https://saramotor.com (“Website”). We want to clarify that we do not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors to our Website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect or process any personal information about visitors to our Website. This includes but is not limited to identifiers such as name, email address, IP address, or location data.

2. California Residents’ Rights
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information, even if we do not collect or process any data.
These rights include:
    •    Right to Know: California residents have the right to request information regarding the categories of personal information a business collects, uses, and shares. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to disclose.
    •    Right to Delete: California residents have the right to request the deletion of personal information collected by businesses. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no information to delete.
    •    Right to Opt-Out of Sale: California residents have the right to opt-out of the sale of their personal information. We do not sell personal information.
    •    Right to Non-Discrimination: We respect your rights under the CCPA and do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any changes in our business practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
